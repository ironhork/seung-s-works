📝 How to Use the TOEIC Vocabulary Quiz

This quiz helps learners practice TOEIC vocabulary through interactive questions. Understanding the features will help you study more effectively.

Basic Quiz Usage

Select your preferred mode (ENG to KOR or KOR to ENG) and the number of questions. Click '학습 시작' to begin your session.

Review and Custom Lists

Use the '틀린 단어 복습' feature to focus on words you missed. You can also create your own vocabulary list using the '나만의 단어장' feature for personalized study.
