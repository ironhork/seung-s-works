📅 30 Day TOEIC Study Plan

Preparing for the TOEIC exam requires a consistent study routine. This 30 day study plan will help you improve vocabulary, listening, and reading skills effectively.

Week 1: Fundamentals

Focus on learning basic TOEIC vocabulary and reviewing common business expressions. Start with the 'Top 500' list.

Week 2: Comprehension

Practice reading comprehension and short conversations. Focus on Part 1, 2, and 5.

Week 3: Mock Tests

Take practice tests and review incorrect answers carefully. Use the 'Review Mode' for vocabulary.

Week 4: Final Push

Focus on speed and accuracy while solving practice questions. Take full-length mock exams under timed conditions.
