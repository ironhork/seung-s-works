💡 TOEIC Exam Tips

Improving your TOEIC score requires effective study strategies. Here are several tips that can help you prepare for the test.

  • Study vocabulary daily: Consistency is key in building a strong vocabulary.
  • Practice listening regularly: Expose yourself to various English accents and business situations.
  • Take mock tests: Familiarize yourself with the test format and time constraints.
  • Review mistakes carefully: Understand why you got a question wrong to avoid repeating the same mistake.
