📘 Complete TOEIC Learning Guide

The TOEIC test evaluates English proficiency in business situations. Understanding the test structure is important for achieving a high score.

Listening Section

Focus on understanding short conversations and workplace dialogues. Practice with various accents (American, British, Australian) as they are all included in the test.

Reading Section

Improve your vocabulary and reading speed through daily practice. Part 7 requires quick scanning and information synthesis across multiple documents.
